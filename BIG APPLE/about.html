About Bigapple Travel Center

Making people Happy with Low prices

Established in 2021, the Bigapple Travel Center has quickly risen to become the premier travel destination in four states, a testament to our commitment to quality and affordability. We pride ourselves on offering the lowest prices in the industry, earning the trust of our valued customers and outshining the competition. Our store is akin to a small Walmart, where you can explore a diverse array of top-quality products, ensuring your every need is met with convenience and value. As we continue to expand and serve our customers with unwavering dedication, the Bigapple Travel Center promises an enduring legacy of excellence.

OUR HISTORY

We envision creating a substantial gas station and truck stop, consisting of 8 individual gas stations merged into one large and comprehensive facility. Our motivation for this project is to provide invaluable support to truckers by offering them access to top-notch amenities, including affordable and high-quality shower facilities, as well as a wide range of delicious food and beverages. Our primary goal is to enhance the overall experience for truckers on the road, making their journeys more comfortable, convenient, and enjoyable.
